# Refund Policy

## Eligibility Window
You may request a refund or return within 30 days of the original purchase date. Orders placed through promotional or clearance sales may have a reduced 14-day window. Custom or personalized items are not eligible for return unless they arrived defective.

## Condition Requirements
To qualify for a refund, items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in the same condition as received. Software or digital products that have been activated or downloaded are non-refundable. For physical goods, we require that all accessories, manuals, and promotional items be included in the return shipment.

## How to Initiate a Return
Log in to your Acme Support account, navigate to Order History, select the item you wish to return, and click "Request Return." You will receive a prepaid return shipping label via email. Print the label, securely pack the item, and drop it off at the carrier location indicated on the label.

## Processing Timeline
Once we receive your returned item, our inspection team will review it within 3-5 business days. If the return meets our condition requirements, the refund will be issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days. You will receive an email confirmation at each stage of the process.

## Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be granted in the following situations: the item is returned with minor cosmetic damage not caused by shipping, the original packaging is missing or damaged, or the return is initiated outside the standard 30-day window but within 45 days (subject to a 20% restocking fee).

## Exceptions and Non-Refundable Items
The following items are non-refundable: gift cards, downloadable software that has been activated, subscription fees for billing periods already consumed, and items marked as final sale. Acme Support reserves the right to refuse a refund if there is evidence of misuse, intentional damage, or failure to follow the return instructions.

## Order Cancellations
Orders may be cancelled free of charge within 1 hour of placement. After 1 hour, cancellation depends on whether the item has shipped. If it has not shipped, a full refund will be issued. If it has shipped, you must follow the standard return process after delivery.
