# Billing Policy

## Subscription Plans
Acme Support offers three subscription tiers: Basic ($19/month), Pro ($49/month), and Enterprise (custom pricing). Each tier provides a different level of support coverage, SLA commitments, and feature access. Plans are billed monthly unless an annual commitment is selected, which provides a 15% discount on the monthly rate.

## Accepted Payment Methods
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payment methods must be authorised for recurring transactions if used for subscription billing. Prepaid cards and virtual cards issued by digital banking platforms are accepted only if they support recurring payments.

## Invoice Generation
Invoices are generated on the first day of each billing period. A PDF invoice is emailed to the primary account holder and is also available for download under Account Settings > Billing History. Each invoice includes a breakdown of line items, applicable taxes, and the payment due date. Enterprise customers receive consolidated monthly invoices covering all sub-accounts.

## Payment Due Dates
Payment is due upon receipt of the invoice. For subscription plans, the payment is processed automatically on the invoice date using the default payment method on file. If the automatic payment fails, a reminder email is sent, and the payment remains due. A grace period of 5 calendar days is provided before any service impact occurs.

## Late Payment Handling
If payment is not received within the 5-day grace period, the account is placed on a 10-day restricted access period during which the dashboard and support portal remain accessible but new ticket creation is suspended. After 10 days of restricted status, the account is suspended, and all active tickets are paused. Reactivation requires full payment of the outstanding balance plus a $15 late reconnection fee. Accounts suspended for more than 60 days may be subject to permanent closure and data deletion.

## Price Changes
Acme Support reserves the right to adjust subscription pricing with 30 days' advance notice via email. Price increases apply at the start of the next billing cycle. Existing annual commitments are honoured at the original rate until the renewal date. Promotional or introductory pricing expires after the stated period and does not qualify for price-lock guarantees.

## Billing Disputes
If you believe you have been billed incorrectly, contact our billing team at billing@acmesupport.com within 60 days of the invoice date. Disputes submitted after 60 days may not be reviewed. While a dispute is under investigation, service will not be interrupted provided the disputed amount is less than 50% of the total invoice.
