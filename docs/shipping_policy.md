# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Methods

We offer three shipping methods: Standard (5-8 business days), Expedited (2-3 business days), and Overnight (next business day by end of day). Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight, destination, and selected method. Orders over $100 qualify for free standard shipping within the continental United States.

## Delivery Timeframes

Delivery times are calculated from the moment your order leaves our warehouse, not from the time of purchase. Orders placed before 2:00 PM local time on business days are typically processed and shipped the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends/holidays are processed on the next business day. Delivery estimates are not guaranteed for international shipments.

## International Shipping

We ship to over 60 countries worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs duties, import taxes, and brokerage fees, which are the responsibility of the recipient. Delivery times for international orders range from 10-20 business days depending on the destination and customs processing. Some products may have shipping restrictions to certain countries due to local regulations.

## Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's tracking page. Tracking information typically becomes available within 24 hours of shipment. You can also view tracking information under Order History in your account dashboard. For orders with multiple items, you may receive separate tracking numbers if items ship from different locations.

## Delayed Shipments

If your order has not shipped within the estimated processing window, contact support for a status update. Weather events, natural disasters, or carrier disruptions may cause delays beyond our control. If a delay exceeds 10 business days past the estimated delivery date, you may cancel for a full refund or request expedited shipping at no additional cost.

## Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package does not arrive within the estimated delivery window, contact our support team within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We will initiate a trace with the carrier. If the package is confirmed lost, a replacement will be shipped or a full refund issued. For damaged items, submit photo evidence within 48 hours of receipt, and we will arrange a replacement or refund.
