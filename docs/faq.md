# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account Management

### How do I create an account?
Visit the Acme Support sign-up page and enter your email address, a strong password, and your full name. You will receive a verification email — click the link inside to activate your account.

### How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password." Enter the email address associated with your account. A password reset link will be sent to your inbox. The link expires after 60 minutes. If you do not see the email, check your spam folder.

### Can I update my email address or profile details?
Yes. Log in and navigate to Account Settings. You can change your name, email address, phone number, and time zone. Changing your email address triggers a new verification email. Your old email remains active until the new one is verified.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Account Settings and select "Delete Account." You will be asked to confirm your password. Deletion is irreversible and removes all personal data, open tickets, and order history. This process may take up to 48 hours to complete.

## Shipping

### What shipping methods are available?
We offer standard shipping (5-8 business days), expedited shipping (2-3 business days), and overnight shipping (next business day). Delivery times are calculated from the moment your order leaves our warehouse.

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes, Acme Support ships to over 60 countries. International orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient. Delivery times for international orders range from 10-20 business days depending on the destination.

### How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's tracking page. You can also view tracking information under Order History in your account dashboard.

### What happens if my package is lost or damaged?
If your package does not arrive within the estimated delivery window, contact our support team within 14 days of the expected delivery date. For damaged items, submit photo evidence within 48 hours of receipt, and we will arrange a replacement or refund.

## Payment Methods

### Which payment methods do you accept?
Acme Support accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and Apple Pay. We also accept debit cards that carry a Visa or Mastercard logo. We do not accept personal checks, money orders, or cash.

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes. All payment transactions are processed through PCI-compliant gateways. Acme Support does not store full credit card numbers on our servers. We use tokenization to securely reference your payment method for recurring billing.

### Why was my payment declined?
Payments may be declined due to insufficient funds, an expired card, a mismatch between the billing address on file and the one provided, or fraud prevention blocks. If your payment is declined, check your card details and try again, or contact your bank.

## Troubleshooting

### The portal is not loading correctly. What should I do?
First, clear your browser cache and cookies. If the issue persists, try a different browser or open an incognito/private window. Acme Support is optimized for the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

### I did not receive a notification email.
Check your spam or promotions folder. Add noreply@acmesupport.com to your address book or safe senders list. If notifications are still not arriving, verify that your email address is correct in Account Settings.

### The chat widget is not appearing.
Ensure that your browser is not blocking JavaScript. Some ad blockers or corporate firewalls may prevent the chat widget from loading. If you are using a VPN, try disabling it temporarily. For persistent issues, contact our technical support team through the help center.
