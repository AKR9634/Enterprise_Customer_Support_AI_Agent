# Product Policy

## Product Specifications

Product specifications are listed on each product's detail page. Specifications include dimensions, weight, materials, compatibility, power requirements, and supported standards. Specifications may vary by region due to local regulations. Contact support if you need specifications not listed on the product page.

## Product Compatibility

Our products are designed to work with industry-standard systems. Compatibility information is provided for each product and includes supported operating systems, hardware requirements, and accessory compatibility. Third-party accessories are not guaranteed to be compatible unless explicitly listed. For enterprise integrations, refer to the product's technical documentation.

## Warranty Coverage

All products come with a manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Standard warranty periods vary by product category — electronics carry a 1-year warranty, accessories carry a 6-month warranty, and software carries a 90-day warranty from the purchase date. The warranty covers repair or replacement at our discretion. Damage caused by misuse, unauthorized modifications, or normal wear and tear is not covered.

## Warranty Claims

To file a warranty claim, contact support with your order number, product serial number, and a description of the issue. You may be asked to provide photo or video evidence. Approved claims will receive a prepaid return shipping label. Once the returned item is received and inspected, a replacement will be shipped within 5-7 business days or a refund will be issued.

## Stock Availability

Product stock levels are updated in real-time on the website. If a product is out of stock, you can sign up for email notifications when it becomes available. Backordered items are typically restocked within 2-4 weeks. Estimated restock dates are listed on the product page when available.

## Inventory Questions

For bulk or wholesale inventory inquiries, contact our B2B sales team at b2b@acmesupport.com. Volume discounts are available for orders of 50+ units. Lead times for bulk orders may differ from standard retail availability.
