# Account Policy

## Password Reset

If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and a password reset link will be sent to your inbox. The link expires after 60 minutes for security reasons. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support to verify your email address is correct.

## Email Verification

New accounts require email verification before full access is granted. Click the verification link sent to your email after registration. If the link expires, request a new one from Account Settings. Changing your email address triggers a new verification — your old email remains active until the new one is verified.

## Account Deletion

To delete your account, go to Account Settings and select "Delete Account." You will be asked to confirm your password. Deletion is irreversible and removes all personal data, open tickets, and order history. The process may take up to 48 hours to complete. Active subscriptions must be cancelled before deletion.

## Address Management

You can add, edit, or remove shipping addresses under Account Settings > Addresses. One address can be set as default, which will be pre-selected during checkout. A minimum of one address is required for placing orders. Address verification may be required for high-value shipments.

## Login Troubleshooting

If you are unable to log in, first verify your email and password are correct. After 5 failed login attempts, your account will be temporarily locked for 30 minutes. If you have enabled two-factor authentication (2FA), ensure your authenticator app is synced correctly. Contact support if the lockout persists after the cooldown period.

## Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account. Once enabled, you will be prompted for a one-time code from your authenticator app in addition to your password during login. To enable 2FA, go to Account Settings > Security and follow the setup instructions. If you lose access to your authenticator app, contact support with your account details to have 2FA reset.
